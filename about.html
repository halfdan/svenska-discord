--- layout: svenska title: Svenska Discord subtitle: Om oss. show_downloads: false --- ## Who are we? We are a mix of language learners/natives/language enthusiasts/linguists/translators/programmers and who knows what else, who like to talk in and about Swedish on our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/FUMTtWJ). If you'd like to contribute to this website, create a Github account then send **sustained** a DM with your username or email address. You'll also need to be a member of the Discord too, of course.
